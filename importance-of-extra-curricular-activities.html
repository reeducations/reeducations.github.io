22. The postive and negative outcomes of extra curricular activities​


Positive outcomes of extra-curricular activities:

1. Students get to explore the different aspects and capacities of their intelligence.

2. Students become more engaged and proactive in school activities.

3. Extra-curricular activities promote socialization and intellectual expansion.

4. Students learn and embody broader aspects of their skills and knowledge.

Negative outcomes of extra-curricular activities:

1. Students tend to be more focused with school activities and neglect their academics.

2. Extra-curricular activities can be demanding in terms of resources, time and effort.  Thus, it can be exhausting.

3. Extra-curricular activities are usually competition-based which can heighten a student's anxiety, conflict and stress.
