The rectangular frame has an area of 4,875 cm. If the length Is 75 cm, what is its width?

Answer:

[tex]\huge \red{ \boxed{ \green{ \sf{65cm}}}}[/tex]

The problem knows the area of the rectangular frame, length has given too. We are finding the width of the rectangular frame.

To get the width of the rectangular shape use the formula W = A/L where you will divide the area given and length given.

Formula: [tex]\boxed{\bold{\orange{W \: = \: \frac{A}{L}}}}[/tex]

Now, solve!

[tex]\tt{W \: = \: \frac{A}{L} }[/tex]

[tex]\tt{W \: = \: \frac{{4,875cm}^{2}}{75cm} }[/tex]

[tex]\tt{W \: = \: \frac{{4,875cm}^{2}}{75cm} }[/tex]

[tex]\tt{W \: = }[/tex] [tex]\tt\underline\green{65cm}[/tex]

The width of the rectangular frame measures 65cm.
