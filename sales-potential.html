9. Refers to the total market sales potential of all companies put together?​


Answer:

Market size is the total market sales potential of all companies put together.

Explanation:

Potential market is the part of the total population that has shown some level of interest in buying a particular product or service. This includes individuals, firms and organizations. Potential market is also called Total addressable market.

The size of a potential market helps a company to understand the level of investment it should make further in the market, while taking into consideration other factors. If the potential market is very small, it means there is a very few fraction of the total population showing any interest in the product or category. This means that it is better that the company doesn’t invest further in the product or category because people won’t buy it. If the size of the potential market is large, it means that there is a huge fraction of the total population which is showing interest in the product, so further investment can be made on the product development or modification, marketing, promotions etc.
