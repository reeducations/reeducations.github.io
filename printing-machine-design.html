5. 1. How is silkscreen printing done?2. How is a digital technology used in silkscreen printing today?3. What is the advantage of using silkscreen printing machine in printing originaldesigns?pasagot please​


1.Screen printing is the process of transferring a stencilled design onto a flat surface using a mesh screen, ink and a squeegee. Fabric and paper are the most commonly screen-printed surfaces, but with specialised inks it's also possible to print onto wood, metal, plastic, and even glass.

2.Screen printing and industrial screen printing use screens, pieces of permeable mesh stretched over a rigid frame, which allow ink to pass through to the fabric below. This method enables fast and high-quality printing, but preparing the screen is an expensive manual process, with a set-up cost which is only absorbed by producing long print runs with the same screen.

++

In digital printing, the machine first applies a solution to the fabric that helps the ink to adhere properly to that particular surface. Technically, this solution is called a “primer” and usually contains acetic acid.

The surface of the fabric is now ready to receive and hold a special type of ink for four-colour printing on textiles. The fabric is then dried with hot air.

3.Advantages include; It's quick and easy to print in full color- Printing directly allows for a full range of colors, whereas screen printing can only allow a single color to be printed with each pressing.
