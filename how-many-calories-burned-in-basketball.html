26. JEFFBURNS 500 CALORIES PER HOUR BICYCLING. HOW LONGEILL HAVE TO RIDE TO BURN 750 CALORIES?​


Answer:

1.5 hours or 1 hour and 30 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

Solving using ratio and proportion. Let x be the time he'll need to burn 750 calories when bicycling.

500 : 1 = 750 : x

(500)(x) = (750)(1)

500x = 750

500x/500 = 750/500

x = 1.5 hours or 1 hour and 30 minutes
