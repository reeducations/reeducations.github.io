1. What is the area if a square that has a length of 56 cm and with of 56 cm?​


The area of the square is calculated by multiplying the length by the width:

Area = Length x Width

Area = 56 cm x 56 cm

Area = 3,136 cm²

Therefore, the area of the square is 3,136 square centimeters.
