9. similarities and differences of american, french, and russian table set up/service​


Answer:

Differences of American,French,and Russian - Russian service traditionally means food delivered to your table on platters and a waiter picks up the item with tools and plates it for you.

French service usually means two waiters are serving you, a front waiter and a back waiter. The front waiter often prepares items tableside, explains details about the food and sometimes assists with wine pairings. The back waiter does more routine maintenance at the table like water glasses and clearing plates.

American service is a less formal approach than French where a single server does just about all the guest service.

Similarties of Russian and French - Service is from the left While French cart service and Russian service both prepare food tableside, in Russian, the food goes on platters for the guests to select their own food, and French cart service the food is placed on individual plates before being brought to the table.

11. A french word meaning to skim or to touch lightly on is a series of massage strokes used in Swedish massage to warm up the muscle​


Answer:

Effleurage

Explanation:

Effleurage, a French word meaning "to skim" or "to touch lightly on", is a series of massage strokes used in Swedish massage to warm up the muscle before deep tissue work using petrissage.

This is a soothing, stroking movement used at the beginning and the end of the facial and/or body massage. It is also used as a linking move between the different strokes and movements. Effleurage is basically a form of massage involving a circular stroking movement made with the palm of the hand.

26. Mr. Francois Renault, a French resident and a citizen is interested in putting up a Travel Agency in the Philippines, specializing in European outbound tour packages. If you are the lawyer appointed by Mr. Renault to assist him in putting up this kind of business, what advice will you give him?


Answer:

As a lawyer, I will advise Mr. Renault to get an Operating Permit from the Department of Tourism (DOT) for his travel agency business. An operating permit is the basic requirement for an establishment to engage in any business activity.

A foreign national may only operate a business in the Philippines under an operating permit issued by the DOT. The operating permit is issued after application and review by the concerned government agency and grant or denial of issuance thereof by the concerned government agency.

The law provides that: "No person shall engage in any business except through an agent duly registered with the Commission." This means that unless you have an agent or representative who is duly registered with DOT, you cannot open up your own travel agency business here in the Philippines.

The Philippine Tour Operators Association has recommended that all travel agencies should register with DOT so as to avail of its services and benefits, including but not limited to: 1) recognition as authorized tour operators; 2) registration with other regulatory bodies like PTOA and ACTOEI; 3) exemption from paying taxes on gross income; 4) exemption from paying fees for issuing permits; 5) exemption from paying fees for renewing permits; 6) renewal of annual registration

29. Give the meaning of augment, embroidery,stitch,french seam,and invert.​


Augment

To make larger, enlarge in size, number, strength, extent, or increase.

Embroidery

The craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn.

Stitch

A loop or portion of thread disposed in place by one such movement in sewing.

French Seam

It's used most commonly on sheer fabric, so the seam blends with the fabric. Encloses the seam allowance on the inside of a sewn item, so no raw edge is visible.

Invert

is made by sewing 2 pieces of fabric, the wrong sides together first, then pressing the seam open and folding the fabric over so the right sides face outwards.
